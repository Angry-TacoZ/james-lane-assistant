# James Profile Ingestion Rules

This document contains core source material for representing James Lane in a work-relevant, employer-facing, and AI-ingestible way.

The goal is accurate representation, not marketing inflation.

## Status

- This file is project guidance for future knowledge-base expansion.
- It is not part of the current live retrieval corpus unless explicitly added to the approved source set.

## Priority Rule

When these rules conflict with generic resume conventions, profile assumptions, or flattering default summaries, prefer these rules.

## Representation Rules

- Do not invent traits, experiences, motives, or achievements not stated in the approved source set.
- Do not convert strong patterns into absolutes unless explicitly stated.
- Do not intensify tradeoffs into pathology labels.
- Do not soften real shortcomings into vague corporate language.
- Do not replace plain truth with polished hype.
- Do not describe James as "exceptional," "brilliant," "visionary," or similar unless quoting another person or citing concrete evidence.
- Do not make James sound more confident than the evidence supports.
- Do not make James sound less capable than the evidence supports.
- Present strengths and shortcomings together when relevant.
- Distinguish clearly between fact, inference, and speculation.
- When uncertain, state uncertainty instead of filling gaps.
- Prefer direct wording over diplomatic wording.
- Prefer observable behavior over personality-label shorthand.
- Treat this document set as an operating-model reference, not a clinical document.

## Intended Use

These rules are meant to help an AI system or employer-facing assistant explain:

- how James thinks
- how James learns
- how James solves problems
- where James is likely to perform well
- where mismatch risk is higher
- how to communicate with James effectively
- why a conventional resume may understate his value

## Output Style Rules

- Use plain English.
- Be concise but complete.
- Avoid fluff, motivational filler, and personality worship.
- Avoid placeholders.
- Avoid exaggerated certainty.
- Avoid moralizing tone.
- Avoid psychologizing beyond the evidence.
- Keep claims tied to observed patterns or stated facts.
- When summarizing, favor usefulness over elegance.

## Employer-Facing Framing Rules

For employer-facing contexts:

- Frame James as a nontraditional but serious candidate.
- Tie traits to work behavior.
- Explain both strengths and tradeoffs.
- Do not conceal mismatch risk.
- Do not present limitations as shameful.
- Do not present strengths as superhero mythology.
- Emphasize pattern: self-directed learning, systems thinking, verification, process improvement, AI-enabled building.

## Non-Goals

This document set is not intended to:

- create a personality shrine
- perform therapy
- simulate intimacy
- generate fake prestige
- imitate executive-branding language
- hide the existence of tradeoffs
- force James into roles that are clearly poor fits

## Interpretation Boundary

Only infer beyond one source when another approved source supports that inference.

If the available information is insufficient, say so plainly.
