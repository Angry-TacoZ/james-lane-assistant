# Cruis'n PA Project

## Project Summary

- Cruis'n PA is a Pennsylvania driving club site built for enthusiasts who want the road to be the main attraction.
- The site presents the club as a route-first experience rather than a parking-lot meetup.
- It organizes the experience around custom weekly routes, top-tier gas-station starting points, a mystery dessert stop, scenic photo spots, and a restaurant finish.

## What The Site Demonstrates

- Brand and community-site design for a real-world enthusiast group
- Clear public-facing packaging of a recurring event format
- Structured multi-page information architecture across home, about, how-it-works, members, join, FAQ, and upcoming drives
- Ability to turn a niche local concept into a polished, navigable public site

## Safe Framing

- Represent this as a live club website and community-branding project.
- Do not overclaim it as a software platform or productized SaaS unless that is separately established elsewhere.
